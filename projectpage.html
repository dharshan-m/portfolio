SMART ROOM





SMART ROOM is an combination of GOOGLE CLASSROOM and MICROSOFT TEAMS.



SMART ROOM provides the private and public MESSENGER, high quality video calls and JAMBOARD.



It has a special feature as, If you having a meet at 12:00 PM you will receive a notification message at 11:59:00 AM and 11:59:30 AM and 12:00:00 PM , From these notification message if you click one of them, YOU will directly taken to the video call.
